About Us

"Welcome to Atithi, where the rich tapestry of India’s culinary heritage comes alive on your plate. We are dedicated to bringing you the authentic flavors of India, crafted with love and tradition. Our chefs skillfully blend a medley of spices and the freshest ingredients to create dishes that are both timeless and contemporary. From the warmth of our tandoor ovens to the aromatic spices that define our cuisine, every meal is a journey through the diverse regions of India. Whether you’re here to enjoy a comforting classic or to explore bold, new tastes, we promise an experience that’s as vibrant and diverse as the country itself. Join us, and let us take you on a culinary adventure that’s rich in flavor, tradition, and warmth."

Our Story

"Our story began with a deep love for the vibrant and diverse cuisine of India. Growing up in a family where food was more than just a meal, it was a way of connecting with our heritage and each other, we were inspired to bring the flavors of our homeland to Ahemdabad. We started as a small family kitchen, sharing traditional recipes passed down through generations, each dish crafted with the same love and care that we experienced at home. As word spread about our authentic flavors and warm hospitality, our humble kitchen grew into the restaurant you see today. Every dish we serve carries a piece of our history, from the bustling markets of Delhi to the serene villages of Kerala. We’ve remained true to our roots, blending the old with the new, to create an experience that honors the past while embracing the present. At Atithi, we invite you to be a part of our story. Whether you’re enjoying a familiar favorite or trying something new, we hope each bite transports you to the heart of India, just as it does for us."
